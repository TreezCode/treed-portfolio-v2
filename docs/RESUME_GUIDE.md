## 🚨 CRITICAL ADVICE: How to Position AI Experience

### ✅ DO:
- Emphasize **self-directed learning** and curiosity
- Connect AI to **practical applications** (generative art, development workflows)
- Show you understand **both the technical and creative** sides
- Mention **specific tools** (Stable Diffusion, Windsurf, MCP)
- Frame it as **embracing the future**, not replacing developers

### ❌ DON'T:
- Overstate your experience (you're exploring, not an AI engineer)
- Make it sound like you only use AI to write code (you use it to enhance workflows)
- Ignore security implications (AI tools can introduce vulnerabilities)
- Forget to mention you understand the fundamentals (AI assists, doesn't replace knowledge)

### Perfect Framing:
> "I'm passionate about AI as a tool for amplification, not replacement. I've explored 
> generative AI for creative applications and integrated AI-assisted workflows to improve 
> my development speed. But I believe the best results come from developers who understand 
> fundamentals deeply AND know how to leverage cutting-edge tools strategically."
